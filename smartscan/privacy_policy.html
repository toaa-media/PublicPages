5. Data Retention

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Local Storage: Documents are stored in the app's private directory, protected by Android's sandboxing.
  • No Cloud Storage: By default, your documents are not uploaded to any cloud storage.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We follow data minimization principles and only collect what is necessary.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
