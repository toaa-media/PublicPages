Common Questions

Find quick answers to everything you need to know about our studio and apps.

What is Perfect World Studio?
We are a boutique Android development studio focused on high-performance utility tools and AI-powered productivity apps. Our mission is to create a "Perfect World" of digital experiences where tools are fast, secure, and beautiful.
Are your apps safe to use?
Highly! We prioritize user privacy with local-first processing. Our Privacy Vault uses industrial-grade AES-256 encryption, and we never upload your sensitive data to any central server.
How does the AI OCR Scanner work?
It leverages advanced machine learning models (on-device) to detect text in images with high precision. It supports multiple languages and processes everything locally on your phone for maximum speed and privacy.
Is the WiFi File Share secure?
Yes, SwiftShare creates a direct Peer-to-Peer (P2P) local connection between devices. No data ever leaves your local network or touches our servers during the transfer, making it faster and safer than cloud storage.
Can I use the apps offline?
Most features, including OCR Scanning and Local File Sharing, are designed to work perfectly without an internet connection. We build our tools to be dependable in any situation.
Do you collect or sell user data?
No. We believe your data belongs to you. Our apps do not track your activity, collect personal information, or sell data to third parties. We generate revenue through optional premium features, not your data.
How do I get the latest updates?
All our official apps are hosted on the Google Play Store. We recommend enabling "Auto-update" in your Play Store settings to ensure you always have the latest security patches and feature enhancements.
Are there any hidden costs?
Our core utility features are entirely free. For power users who need advanced capabilities, we offer 'Pro' versions via a single one-time purchase or clear subscription models—no hidden tricks.
What should I do if I find a bug?
We love feedback! If you encounter an issue or have a feature request, please use the 'Contact Support' link in the footer or email us directly at support@perfectworld.studio. We respond to every user message.
Do you support older Android versions?
We strive for broad compatibility. Most of our tools support Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above. For some AI features, a more modern version of Android (11+) might be required for optimal performance.
Where are you located?
Perfect World Studio is a global distributed team of developer-artists and designers. Our core mission is to bring high-quality, beautiful, and functional software to millions of users worldwide.
